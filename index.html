“Yoga is not just about physical activity. It’s a holistic practice that addresses our physical, mental, and spiritual health as a whole.

In Ashtanga Yoga, there are eight aspects in total. Aside from Asanas, there are breathing techniques to help regulate our prana or life energy.

We call this Pranayama. There’s also mediation or Dhyana, philosophy, and guidelines on ethical conduct. Practising Yoga will improve our self-awareness and help us to confront our inner selves with honesty.

Our focus today is on Asanas – the physical postures. Asanas improve our physical health and ability to concentrate. We become more aware of our body, we recognize our individual abilities and limitations. Join our yoga class to experience the benefits of yoga for yourself!”
